Our Mission

We make people gather over chai, coffee or drinks, watch matches, and support their teams. To build interest-based communities & provide pleasant experiences using technology. To develop an inclusive culture & be the go-to global platform for people to explore interest-based experiences.

Soccer is played at all levels throughout the world from small kids leagues to professional and international teams. Perhaps the most famous soccer tournament is the World Cup. Held every four years, the World Cup is a soccer competition among countries and is one of the most watched events in the world. One of the reasons soccer is so popular is that it really only takes a ball and a flat open area to play. Kids throughout the world will make up fields and goals just about anywhere and start playing the game. The game is also fun and competitive.
